Recipe for Lasagna

Back to Recipes

description

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat; drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Add half of the ricotta mixture, followed by half of the mozzarella cheese and some marinara sauce.
  8. Repeat layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and sauce.
  9. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
